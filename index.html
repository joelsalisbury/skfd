About Us

The South Killingly Fire Department exists to Protect the Citizens of the District from Emergencies and Disasters through Fire Suppression, Emergency Medical Services, Hazardous Material Mitigation, Fire Prevention, and Public Education Programs.﻿

The town of Killingly is 50 square miles and consists of approximately 18,000 people. The town is covered by 6 Fire Departments and one ambulance corps:

  • Danielson Fire Department
  • Dayville Fire Department﻿
  • Attawaugan Fire Department﻿
  • Williamsville Fire Department﻿
  • East Killingly Fire Department﻿
  • South Killingly Fire Department
  • KB Ambulance

The town's emergency services are dispatched by Quinebaug Valley Emergency Communications.

The South Killingly Fire Department serves the District of South Killingly in the town of Killingly. It covers approximately 21 square miles.

South Killingly is an unmanned volunteer station housing an Engine, Engine-Tanker, Brush Truck, Light Rescue, Boat, UTV and Technical Rescue Trailer.

The fire district board of directors meets every month on the 3rd Tuesday at 7pm at the fire station. All South Killingly fire taxpayers are welcome to attend if they have comments questions or concerns.
